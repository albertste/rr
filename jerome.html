Q&A

Hey Jerome. Thanks for being on R&R. Why did you join?

But I gave it a second thought. It might help with the time-consuming aspects of meeting someone new. I guess we'll see how it goes!

What does a typical day for you look like?

Work in the mornings and sports and social activities in the afternoons. I travel for both work and leisure.

What are your hobbies?

I play padel and do some weightlifting.

I also read a lot. A big part of my job is to keep an open mind and stay on top of how the world works so I can make good investments.

Chemistry. Sharing a sense of humor. I think everything else is secondary.

Ideally younger but I'm flexible.

Caring, laughter, communication.
